🏠 Our Parlor

Crafted Fresh,
Served with Joy.

At Scoops & Smiles, we believe that ice cream is more than a treat—it's a moment shared. Since 2010, our family has been handcrafting every single batch right here in our little parlor, using only the finest seasonal fruits, farm-fresh dairy, and spices sourced from trusted local farmers.

We never cut corners. No artificial colours, no preservatives—just pure, honest ingredients combined with traditional churning techniques passed down through generations. Whether it's a classic Vanilla Bean or our signature Alphonso Mango, every scoop tells a story.
